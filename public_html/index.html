Our Games

Play some of our best browser games. They are all free. The games listed below are either Alpha or Beta version. If you want to help with, or test unfinished/pre-alpha games, go to Random.

Lemontactics!

Lemontactics! is a tactical RPG where you control cute Raccoons (Or Boy Scouts) in order to capture the enemy base, namely the Lemonade Stand. Bring your units into epic wars filled with Traps, Towers and much more!

Play »

Lemontastic! BETA

Lemontastic! is an incremental game with a moderate amount of action. You manage a modern lemonade stand and strive to attract as many customers as you can. Stand out amongst others, collect lemons, modify or add a few secret ingredients to improve your mixture, and monopolize upon the beverage industry.

Play »

Casino ALPHA

The future Casino from Lemontastic is in development! Play various minigames like Lottery or Slot Machine. Rob the bank! Get expelled! Get a loan! Kill yourself to get out of debt... And much more! Coming soon...

Play »

Flohmarkt ALPHA

A game in collaboration with Sizzi Entertainment. Get scrap and various other useless trash. Did I say useless? Everything has value in Flohmarkt! Break it, glue it, refurbish it, craft it, and finally sell it. Sell everything you make and trade your items in the Flea Market. Coming soon...

Play »

Space Fugitive ALPHA

Escaping from a fearsome enemy. Your spaceship must survive explosions and bullets from everywhere. Be realistic. Those aircrafts are there to kill you. Use your weapons, charge them, get power ups and keep gathering points. The space is yours! Coming soon...
